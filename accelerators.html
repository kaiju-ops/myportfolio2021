Solutions Dynamic & Smart

The customer insight we gathered helped us shape our focus in designing a new approach to the context menu experience in edge today. This is a breakdown of what we found would be the best options in solving this problem.

Dynamic

Build an accelerator framework into the context menu that will allow for more dynamic content to be displayed in-line.

This will help to bring information closer to the user, and quickly interact with content.

Responsive

Besides reconfiguring the IA, to further help with large, overwhelming context menus, we can experiment with a lightweight mini context menu.

Clicking on "..More actions" will open access to the full context menu.

Invocation

Currently, the only way to invoke the context menu is on right click or press & hold. This can be troublesome for a product such as edge to assume people have this type of muscle memory.

With the new framework, the lightweight mini menu is so subtle, we can introduce new ways of evoking the menu by highlighting text & double clicking on text and other easily accesible interaction models.
